Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical headshot session take?

Our standard sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfit changes. Rush sessions can be completed in as little as 15 minutes while still maintaining our high-quality standards.

What should I wear for my headshot?

We recommend solid colors that complement your skin tone and industry standards. Avoid busy patterns or bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide detailed guidance upon booking.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Standard delivery is within 3-5 business days. Rush service is available with delivery in 24-48 hours. All photos are professionally retouched and delivered in both high-resolution and web-optimized formats.

Do you offer group corporate rates?

Yes! We offer special packages for corporate teams of 5 or more, including on-site photography at your office. Contact us for custom quotes and scheduling options.

What's included in the final package?

Each session includes professional retouching, multiple poses, format variations for different platforms (LinkedIn, website, print), and a commercial usage license. Additional retouching services available upon request.
